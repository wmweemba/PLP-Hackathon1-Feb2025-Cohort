About Me

Hey there! I’m William Mweemba, a tech enthusiast, problem solver, and lifelong learner who’s always eager to explore new technologies.
With over a decade of experience across the service, banking, and financial sectors, I’ve had the opportunity to work on transformative IT
projects, from core banking migrations to mobile banking rollouts. Throughout my career, I’ve worn many hats—system administrator,
infrastructure manager, network specialist—all of which have strengthened my ability to bridge the gap between business needs and technical
solutions.

But if we’re being honest—I always wanted to be a developer. Now, as part of the PLP Feb 2025 Cohort, I’m making that dream a reality!
I love experimenting with new tools, hacks, and practices to see what works (and what doesn’t), and I’m especially fascinated by AI and
how it can be applied to solve real-world problems. My approach to technology is hands-on—I believe the best way to understand something
is to test, break, and refine it until you unlock its full potential.

I thrive in dynamic environments where innovation and creativity fuel problem-solving. Whether it’s optimizing business processes,
implementing security best practices, or building intuitive user experiences, I’m always up for a challenge. When I’m not deep-diving into the latest
tech trends, you’ll probably find me tinkering with new ideas, refining my skills, or helping businesses leverage technology for growth.
